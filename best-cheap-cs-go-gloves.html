What are the Factors that Affect CS:GO Glove Prices?

Several factors influence the cost of CS:GO gloves, making some pairs more affordable than others. Here are a few key reasons:

  • Condition : CS:GO gloves come in various conditions ranging from Battle-Scarred to Factory New. Gloves in better condition are often more expensive, while those in worse condition tend to be cheaper.
  • Rarity and Supply : Among the main determinants of glove price in CS:GO is its rarity and the supply-demand ratio. More common gloves are cheaper due to their higher supply, and rarer items cost more.
  • Case Availability : Gloves are obtained from opening specific glove cases. If a case containing certain gloves is no longer dropping in the game, the gloves can become more expensive due to limited supply. On the other hand, gloves from cases that are widely available tend to be cheaper.

In addition, it is worth pointing out that another key factor to consider when in the market for cheap CS:GO gloves is the platform on which they are sold. FAQ

What are CS:GO gloves?

Gloves in CS:GO are cosmetic items available in different models, patterns, and rarities. Players can receive glove skins from certain in-game cases, which require keys to open, or they can trade with other players or purchase them on the Steam Community Market.

How do the prices of CS:GO gloves vary across different platforms?

Oftentimes, third-party platforms offer CS:GO gloves for lower prices compared to those on Steam Community Market. It’s because Steam does not let users withdraw their money and takes a portion of the selling as platform fees.

What are the best gloves in CS:GO?

Among the best gloves in CS:GO, which stand out from the rest yet still are rather affordable, are Moto Gloves | Blood Pressure and Moto Gloves | Turtle. Check out our list of the best cheap CS:GO gloves to find out more.

What is the cheapest pair of gloves in CS:GO?

As of May 2023, the cheapest pair of gloves in CS:GO are Driver Gloves | Racing Green in Battle-Scarred condition, which costs $55. In Factory New condition, Hydra Gloves | Mangrove are the cheapest with the price of $399.

What are the odds of getting gloves in CS:GO?

According to the public data that Valve released in 2017, the odds of getting gloves by unboxing a CS:GO case is around 0.25%. It means that one pair of gloves is unboxed from only one of approximately 400 cases opened. Also, note that not all CS:GO cases contain gloves as Rare Special Items, so be mindful when picking a case to open.
