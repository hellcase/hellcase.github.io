Brief Overview of CS:GO Stickers

Weapons are canvases waiting to be adorned with vibrant colors and intricate designs. CS:GO stickers are more than just a tool to do that. With them, you can express your personality on the server, support your favorite professional players and teams, and pay homage to the game’s history and popular memes.

There are various types and rarities of stickers, and we covered everything you need to know about these sticky masterpieces in our article. Today, however, we will present the list of the top CS:GO stickers Valve added to the game in 2023.

Best CS:GO Stickers in 2023

Last year, Valve released two sticker collections: The Boardroom Sticker Capsule and the 10-Year Birthday Sticker Capsule.

The latter introduced a new type of sticker that pushed the boundaries of what was possible before. These are Lenticular stickers, and we truly consider them the best CS:GO stickers of the past year.

Valve released six first-ever lenticular stickers within the 10-Year Birthday Sticker Capsule in August 2023. They quickly became game-changers and gained huge popularity among the CS:GO players. All these stickers are community-made cosmetics created by TheDanidem, Madara, and oKeyush.

The secret of these stickers is an additional texture layer, lenticular film, that creates a “lens effect”. Lenticular stickers have a unique, animated effect that changes as the weapon they are applied to is inspected from different angles. All of them are the first Extraordinary regular stickers. Let’s take a closer look at these stickers and find out what is so special about each.

Skin Lover (Lenticular)

This sticker depicts a CS:GO agent inspecting a Desert Eagle skin with a magnifying glass. When the sticker is rotating, the image within the glass moves, creating an illusion of the agent moving this thing left and right.

Skin Lover (Lenticular) is created by Madara. You can scrape this sticker to change the image and the text on the sticker.

The price of Skin Lover (Lenticular) starts at $2.10.

Freeze (Lenticular)

Freeze! This CT agent is quite frightening, isn’t he? When you inspect the Freeze (Lenticular), the image reflecting in the agent’s tactical goggles’ lenses moves around. Does anyone want to guess the map?

When you scrape Freeze (Lenticular) , the image will change, and the text will say “Freak”.

The lowest price for Freeze (Lenticular) created by Madara is $2.20.

DJ Safecracker (Lenticular)

If the DJ at your party is not Safecracker Voltzmann, don’t even bother to invite us. When you inspect this sticker, the audio visualizer bars move, and it looks like the music is at full blast! Imagine how cool it would be, though, if the Safecracker’s hand also moved the turntable on the DJ setup.

This one is created by oKeyush. If you scrape DJ Safecracker (Lenticular) enough, only the background text will stay.

You can get DJ Safecracker (Lenticular) for around $5.30.

TV Installation (Lenticular)

This glossy paper-based sticker is created by oKeyush. The author depicted a CT agent sitting in front of many old TVs. On eight of them, the picture on the screens changes when the sticker is inspected. You can spot logos of various CS:GO maps on them, such as Inferno, Nuke, and Cache.

When you scrape TV Installation (Lenticular) , cracks appear on every TV screen, and the picture darkens.

Right now, TV Installation (Lenticular) costs $6.30.

Global TV (Lenticular)

If you play CS:GO, you should have used GOTV at least a few times. This system allows you to watch both demos and live matches in-game. This sticker, created by TheDanidem, is the author’s “little homage” to GOTV and SourceTV.

When you scrape Global TV (Lenticular) , on top of the TV “BROADCASTING THE BEST ESPORTS” inscription will appear, and the “GOtv” icon will change to “source tv”. Also, the screen will become broken, and white strips will appear on both hands and arms of the character.

The price of the Global TV (Lenticular) starts at $8.70.

Magic Rush Ball (Lenticular)

If you ever struggled to decide which site to rush, and whether you should play a force buy round or do an eco, Magic Rush Ball (Lenticular) has you covered. TheDanidem, this sticker’s creator, depicted five T agents looking into the magic ball that says “WHERE ARE WE RUSHING?”. Each time you inspect the sticker, the ball will “randomly” display “A” or “B”.

Also, if you scrape this sticker enough, the text will change to “WHAT ARE WE BUYING?” and on the lower part of the ball, inscriptions “A – FULL ECO” and “B – FORCE BUY” will appear.

The legend says that if you apply this sticker to your P90, the magic ball will always tell you to rush B. Magic Rush Ball (Lenticular) created by is the most expensive Lenticular sticker so far, and it costs around $25.

Other Popular CS:GO Stickers Ranked Cheap to Most Expensive

Some of the other stickers released past year are still attention-worthy, even though none of them could beat the coolness of the Lenticular stickers. Here, we assembled a list of the eight best CS:GO stickers from the 10-Year Birthday Sticker Capsule and The Boardroom Sticker Capsule.

We all love memes, and This Is Fine (T) sticker was inspired by one of the most popular ones. Instead of the brown dog, the author depicted a T agent sitting in the middle of the fire flames. Hey, this is you when the score is 1:15!

Added: February, 2022.

Capsule: The Boardroom Sticker Capsule.

This sticker will look great on skins fiery skins like AWP | Wildfire. This Is Fine (T) sells for $0.20.

Green’s Problem

“Green, Green, what’s your problem, Green?” This sticker commemorates the famous clip of a raging streamer who got banned for team damage.

Added: August, 2022.

Capsule: 10-Year Birthday Sticker Capsule.

You can match Green’s Problem with green or yellow skins. Its price is around $0.20.

Baby Medusa

Little cute Baby Medusa! We think it is pretty obvious that the best skin to match this sticker with is AWP | Medusa, but you can come up with your ideas.

Added: August, 2022.

Capsule: 10-Year Birthday Sticker Capsule.

The price of Baby Medusa starts at $0.80.

Romanov’s Fire (Foil)

If you struggle with performing well on the T side, get Dr. Romanov as your little good luck charm. In Romanov’s Fire (Foil), the agent is depicted against the background of fire from the molotov cocktail bottle.

Added: August, 2022.

Capsule: 10 Year Birthday Sticker Capsule.

The price of Romanov’s Fire (Foil) is $1.10.

The Real MVP (Foil)

The Real MVP (Foil) – for all the G.O.A.T.s out there, including you. The color scheme of this sticker allows the creation of great combinations with yellow, green, and white or grey gun skins.

Added: February, 2022.

Capsule: The Boardroom Sticker Capsule.

The price of The Real MVP (Foil) starts at $1.30.

T Rush (Holo)

We all know there is no better strategy than rushing. T Rush (Holo) is a great sticker for those who like playing fast and aggressively. If this is about you, get tips on your playstyle in our article.

Added: February, 2022.

Capsule: The Boardroom Sticker Capsule.

Our advice would be to match this sticker with purple skins. You can get T Rush (Holo) for $2.70.

Showdown (Foil)

If you think the most epic CS:GO battles happen at the Major events, you’re wrong. The most epic battle of the game is happening between creatures from the AWP | Dragon Lore and M4A4 | Howl, and Showdown (Foil) depicts them fighting.

Added: August, 2022.

Capsule: 10 Year Birthday Sticker Capsule.

Fortunately, this sticker does not cost as much as the skins it is dedicated to. Showdown (Foil) will cost you around $7.

Infinite Diamond (Holo)

Infinite Diamond (Holo) magnifies. This sticker will make a great combo with pearlescent skins from the Printstream family and look solid applied to any other full-white skin.

Added: February, 2022.

Capsule: The Boardroom Sticker Capsule.

You can get Infinite Diamond (Holo) for $12.
