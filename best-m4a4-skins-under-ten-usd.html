Brief Overview

In Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, M4A4 is an assault rifle exclusively available to players of the Counter-Terrorist side in-game.

In your arsenal, you can equip M4A1-S instead of M4A4 and vice versa. If you prefer the silenced variant of this rifle, explore the best M4A1-S skins under $10.

M4A4 is a great weapon for middle-range and long-range duels. This rifle offers decent accuracy and a moderate recoil that is easy to control. It is recommended to fire it in small bursts.

M4A4 | Desolate Space

“We’ve lost contact”.

  • Rarity: Classified
  • Finish Style: Custom Paint Job
  • Float value range: 0.00 to 1.00
  • StatTrak™ is available: yes
  • Starting price: $4.97

M4A4 | Desolate Space is a community-made weapon finish designed by DryDoctorEgg and Andy. Valve released this skin in the Gamma Case in June, 2016.

Inspired by the sci-fi culture of the 1970s, this skin features a custom-painted skeleton in a spacesuit with a transparent visor at the background of a blue and purple landscape. There are purple tentacles on the handle and magazine, and some of them are holding a rocket depicted on the weapon’s handguard.

There are StatTrak™ variants of M4A4 | Desolate Space , but none of them fit our ten dollars budget. You can get the basic skin in Well-Worn condition for $5.35 or Field-Tested for $6.25.

  • Rarity: Restricted
  • Finish Style: Custom Paint Job
  • Float value range: 0.00 to 0.80

For the first time, Valve released M4A4 | Griffin alongside Operation Vanguard in November, 2014. It was redesigned a month later to copyright infringement but not graded as Contraband.

The hand-painted motif of a griffin is depicted on the central part of the weapon’s body. The background of the magazine is light teal, and there are a lot of feathers all over the painting.

M4A4 | Griffin is an affordable skin for this assault rifle. The prices start at $1.85, and you can buy a StatTrak™ Minimal Wear skin for $8.60.

M4A4 | Evil Daimyo

“Carmen. I found him…- The Oni and The Valkyrie Part 1”.

  • Rarity: Restricted
  • Finish Style: Custom Paint Job
  • Float value range: 0.00 to 0.52
  • StatTrak™ is available: yes
  • Starting price: $2.05

M4A4 | Evil Daimyo is a weapon finish released within the Falchion Case when Operation Bloodhound started in May, 2015. It was designed by The Honey Badger.

Daimyos were powerful feudal lords in Japan during the feudal era and vassals of the shogun, who was the military ruler of Japan during this period. The author of this Japanese-styled skin adorned it with a red and black geometrical pattern and added a few details, such as icons of a samurai and a triangular shield with crossed katanas.

Battle-Scarred variants of M4A4 | Evil Daimyo sell for a minimum of $2.05, and Factory New ones – for $5.80. You can also get a StatTrak™ Minimal Wear version for $8.65.

M4A4 | In Living Color

“Fight for one, fight for all”.

  • Rarity: Covert
  • Finish Style: Custom Paint Job
  • Float value range: 0.00 to 0.79
  • StatTrak™ is available: yes
  • Starting price: $5.57

M4A4 | In Living Color is an eye-catching skin from the Snakebite Case released after Operation Broken Fang ended in May, 2021. It is a community-made finish designed by kadzor and Nannou.

So much color will surely make you stand out on the server. This skin has a graffiti-styled pattern in vivid neon hues. On the weapon’s body, the author depicted a pink-haired woman in a yellow jacket and white-and-orange protective face mask. There are a lot of details in form of small paintings of round faces, cats, and icons.

M4A4 | In Living Color is not the cheapest skin, but you can get it in Battle-Scarred condition for $5.65, Well-Worn for $8.80, or Field-Tested for $7.50. Yep, no StatTrak™ variants under $10.

With a $10 budget, you can go for M4A4 | Neo-Noir in Battle-Scarred, Well-Worn, or Field-Tested conditions that sell from $4.75 to $6.35. Also, you can get a StatTrak™ Battle-Scarred version for $9.85.
