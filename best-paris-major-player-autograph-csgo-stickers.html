What are Player Autograph Stickers?

Players’ Autograph Stickers are in-game collectible items that Valve had been releasing prior to every Major tournament, starting with ESL One: Cologne 2015 , and the upcoming one is not an exception.

Every player whose team qualifies for the Major gets an Autograph sticker. Although most players design their stickers themselves, Valve sometimes creates them. It happens if players make controversial entries unsuitable for release for various reasons.

Prior to Major, these stickers get sorted in capsules based on the stage of the Major the player qualifies for: Contenders, Challengers, and Legends. After the Grand Final concludes and the Major winner is determined, the Champions Sticker Capsules are also added to CS:GO with the victorious team logo and the players’ signatures.

How to Get Player Autograph Stickers?

You can buy and unbox a Paris 2023 Contenders, Challengers, or Legends Autograph Sticker Capsule to get Player Autograph Stickers. You do not need a key to open these capsules; they are obtainable from the game’s main menu, and you can get an infinite amount of them.

If you are not into opening containers or know precisely which Paris 2023 Autograph Sticker you want to add to your collection, you can head to Steam Community Market and purchase it from other users if there are active offers.

The only other way to acquire Paris 2023 Player Autograph Stickers is to get a Viewer Pass in-game, redeem or purchase a Souvenir Package, and unbox it. A weapon skin from the corresponding collection will drop with one pre-applied signature sticker. Remember that you can not remove this sticker and re-apply it to another weapon.
