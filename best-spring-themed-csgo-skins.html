Sawed-Off | Morris

“Close quarters nouveau”.

  • Rarity: Mil-spec.
  • Finish Style: Patina.
  • Float value range: 0.00 to 1.00.
  • StatTrak™ is available: yes.
  • Starting price: $0.10.

Sawed-Off | Morris was added to the game in September, 2017. It is a community-created weapon finish made by iamrexar.

This is a great CS:GO skin to welcome the season of renewal! The pattern that complements the Sawed-Off’s receiver consists of white lilies and green leaves. The color palette of this shotgun skin is pretty pale, but it surely suits the early days of spring spirit.

Sawed-Off | Morris in a Factory New condition with a StatTrak™ feature costs only $1. If you want a darker finish, go for higher float values. From Battle-Scarred to Field-Tested, this skin sells for $0.10.

Glock-18 | Ironwork

“Left unchecked, any garden becomes a tangled mass of weeds”.

  • Rarity: Mil-Spec.
  • Finish Style: Patina.
  • Float value range: 0.00 to 1.00.
  • StatTrak™ is available: yes.
  • Starting price: $0.15.

The Mil-Spec Glock-18 | Ironwork was designed by L’grand. Valve added this skin to CS:GO within the Glove Case released in March, 2019.

While most of the pistol’s components remained untouched, its slide is embellished with etched patterns of ivy’s intertwining branches and leaves. Also, it has a “GLOCK 18” inscription engraved on top of the slide.

This skin is available in all conditions. The Battle-Scarred variants of Glock-18 | Ironwork only cost $15, and Factory New ones sell for $1.10. You can get an FN StatTrak™ version of this pistol skin for $4.99.

SSG 08 | Spring Twilly

“Last chance; train’s leaving the station”.

  • Rarity: Mil-Spec.
  • Finish Style: Hydrographic.
  • Float value range: 0.00 to 0.55.
  • StatTrak™ is available: no.
  • Starting price: $0.25.

Added in September, 2021, SSG 08 | Spring Twilly is a part of The 2021 Train Collection. This is a Valve-made skin released alongside Operation Riptide’s start.

Painted in black, white, and different hues of green, this skin is complemented with an abstract-styled hydrographic pattern detailed with icons, lightning bolts, and geometric forms. The back part of the sniper rifle’s buttstock is solid black, and its scope is mostly white.

SSG 08 | Spring Twilly is a very cheap weapon finish, and it is available in all conditions. A Battle-Scarred variant sells for $0.30, but a Factory New skin will not hurt your wallet, as it’s only $0.95.

AUG | Fleet Flock

“Some birds represent harmony and peace… these are not those birds”.

  • Rarity: Classified.
  • Finish Style: Custom Paint Job.
  • Float value range: 0.00 to 1.00.
  • StatTrak™ is available: yes.
  • Starting price: $1.90.

Created by PoignantEnvy, AUG | Fleet Flock was added to CS:GO as a part of the Chroma 3 Case at the end of April, 2016.

This primarily dark blue AUG skin is complemented with clouds and swallows, the migratory birds which return home from the South every spring. Some of the gun’s components, such as the magazine, the barrel, and a part of the body, are painted in rainee color.

AUG | Fleet Flock looks great in all conditions, including the lower floats of Battle-Scarred. You can get a Well-Worn skin for around $2, a Factory New variant will cost you $8.10. The lowest price for this skin with a StatTrak™ feature is $3.

Nova | Bloomstick

  • Rarity: Classified.
  • Finish Style: Custom Paint Job.
  • Float value range: 0.06 to 0.80.
  • StatTrak™ is available: yes.
  • Starting price: $5.30.

The custom-painted Nova | Bloomstick first appeared in CS:GO within the eSports 2014 Summer Collection that Valve added in July, 2014. This case is now discontinued.

On the vibrant red base coat of the shotgun’s receiver and buttstock, an airbrushed cheery blossom motif was depicted. There are details like magnificent flowers with pink petals on the branches and leaves. The other components of the weapon are untouched.

This Nova skin is available in all conditions, but it’s not worth getting Battle-Scarred and Well-Worn variants. If you really like this skin and you are ready to spend $75, go for a Factory New skin. A Minimal Wear variant looks decent as well: it will cost you around $15.

Get your Nova | Bloomstick from the Classified Case on Hellcase.

AK-47 | Ice Coaled

“Cold to the touch”.

  • Rarity: Classified.
  • Finish Style: Custom Paint Job.
  • Float value range: 0.00 to 0.77.
  • StatTrak™ is available: yes.
  • Starting price: $10.53.

Created by REINCARNATION, AK-47 | Ice Coaled was released by Valve as a part of the Recoil Case in July, 2022.

This AK-47 is complemented with a custom-painted gradient pattern in bright hues of green and blue. It looks like the winter and spring seasons are changing! The finish is detailed with dots, icons, and inscriptions. A part of the magazine is “transparent”, and some of the bullets are visible.

A Well-Worn AK-47 | Ice Coaled costs $15.90, and a Factory New skin will cost you almost $45. The starting price for a StatTrak™ variant is $20.95.

Claim your AK-47 | Ice Coaled from Recoil Case or KRL Case on Hellcase.

AWP | Corticera

  • Rarity: Classified.
  • Finish Style: Custom Paint Job.
  • Float value range: 0.06 to 0.30.
  • StatTrak™ is available: yes.
  • Starting price: $16.90.

AWP | Corticera is a part of the eSports 2014 Summer Case which was released in July, 2014. Valve made this skin.

This skin’s body has a dark blue base complemented with a green and yellow corticera tree branches motif outlined in bold black. The other parts of the AWP are untouched. Also, the pattern is detailed with little flowers and leaves.

There are no AWP | Corticera skins in Well-Worn and Battle-Scarred conditions. The highest float value this finish can be is 0.30, meaning the worst condition possible is Field-Tested, which costs $16.90. Factory New skins sell for less than $40.

On Hellcase, AWP | Corticera drops from Classified Case , eSports 2014 Case, and many others.

AK-47 | Hydroponic

“Notice how Gunsmith Yukako used the wood of the AK-47 to accentuate her verdant theme… make no mistake: Hydroponic is as beautiful and deadly as nature itself – Imogen, Arms Dealer In Training”.

  • Rarity: Classified.
  • Finish Style: Anodized Multicolored.
  • Float value range: 0.00 to 0.80.
  • StatTrak™ is available: no.
  • Starting price: $615.25.

AK-47 | Hydroponic is a Valve-made skin, and they released it within the Rising Sun Collection when Operation Bloodhound started in May, 2015.

The assault rifle’s body and magazine are coated with white metallic paint, and some of the parts are painted in vivid bright green. The wooden components are default. The pattern consists of bamboo branches.

This skin is quite popular but rare, and it’s pricey. A Battle-Scarred skin will cost you $615, and Minimal Wear variants sell for $1,800.

AK-47 | Hydroponic awaits you in the Indomitable Case on Hellcase!

MP9 | Wild Lily

“Stop and smell the cordite”.

  • Rarity: Classified.
  • Finish Style: Custom Paint Job.
  • Float value range: 0.00 to 0.70.
  • StatTrak™ is available: no.
  • Starting price: $1,200.

MP9 | Wild Lily is a bright-colored SMG skin from the St. Marc Collection released when Operation Shattered Web started in November, 2019.

A motif of blue lilies flowers was painted on the MP9’s dark blue and black base. Pink and purple leaves complement the pattern. The design was completed with geometric details in the form of lines, and the barrel is solid blue.

MP9 | Wild Lily is an extremely rare skin, and it’s sought after. There are no active offers for this skin In Factory New and Minimal Wear conditions on the Steam Community Market. Battle-Scarred variants sell for $1,200 and Field-Tested – for $1,480.

AK-47 | Wild Lotus

“Muck or nettles”.

  • Rarity: Covert.
  • Finish Style: Custom Paint Job.
  • Float value range: 0.00 to 1.00.
  • StatTrak™ is available: no.
  • Starting price: $7,400.

The custom-painted AK-47 | Wild Lotus comes from the St. Marc Collection released alongside Operation Shattered Web in November, 2019.

The pattern of this Covert AK-47 skin is embellished with different-sized hand-painted lotuses in pink and orange colors, green leaves, and beige stripes.

AK-47 | Wild Lotus is one of the most expensive and well-liked skins for this assault rifle, and it is available in all conditions.

There are no active offers for this finish on Steam Community Market right now, but you can find this skin on third-party websites. Battle-Scarred finishes sell for around $2,200, and prices of Factory New variants start at $9,300.
