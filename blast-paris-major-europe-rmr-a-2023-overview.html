About the European RMR A

LAST.tv Paris 2023 RMREurope A is one of the four events of the BLAST.tv Paris Major 2023 qualification. The tournament format is as follows:

  • 16 team Swiss System format
  • Elimination and advancement matches are Best-of-3
  • All other matches are Best-of-1
  • The 4th place decider is resolved via a Best-of-3 single-elimination match
  • Top 8 teams proceed to the BLAST.tv Paris Major
  • The next three teams proceed to the European RMR Decider
  • The bottom 5 teams are eliminated
