Reptile-Themed CS:GO Weapon Skins

This is the second part of our animal-themed CS:GO skins list. In the first part, we gathered all the CS:GO skins with dragons.

Reptiles are a diverse group of cold-blooded animals that includes snakes, lizards, and crocodiles. Each species possesses a unique set of characteristics and behaviors that make each of them as fascinating as they are deadly. Snakes are masters of stealth and precision hunting due to their elongated bodies and flexible jaws.

With their colorful skin and agile movements, lizards are the ultimate opportunists, able to climb, swim, and run with equal skill. And crocodiles, with their ancient, dinosaur-like appearance and raw power, are the ultimate apex predator.

We will cover eight CS:GO skins with reptiles. With these skins equipped, your chances of surviving in the jungle forests will undoubtedly increase (especially in Ancient).

Here’s the list of the best reptile-themed skins for CS:GO weapons:

  • AWP | Snake Camo
  • AWP | Atheris
  • XM1014 | Incinegator
  • USP-S | Caiman
  • P250 | See Ya Later
  • AUG | Chameleon
  • M4A1-S | Welcome to the Jungle
  • AWP | Desert Hydra.

We could not find any skins for knives to put here, but we included Broken Fang Gloves and Hydra Gloves as well as two other skins for gloves on our list. Let’s begin!

AWP | Snake Camo

Rarity: Mil-Spec

Finish Style: Spray-Paint

Float value range: 0.06 to 0.80

StatTrak™: no

Price (MW): $96

AWP | Snake Camo is one of the oldest Valve-made skins in CS:GO, it was released alongside The Arms Deal update in August, 2013. AWP’s body is painted in light fawn beige, and the pattern of this finish was spray-painted with a stencil to imitate the skin of a snake. If you are not a fan of sparkly skins, this is the one to go for.

AWP | Atheris

“As deadly as it is beautiful.”

Rarity: Restricted

Finish Style: Custom Paint Job

Float value range: 0.00 to 1.00

StatTrak™: yes

Price (FN): $15

AWP | Atheris , created by Graff, was added to Counter-Strike: Global Offensive within The Prisma Collection in March 2019. The skin depicts a green and blue African bush viper Atheris crawling through the black-painted body of the sniper rifle with its long tongue out. You can sport a dark green pattern of snake scales on the scope of the AWP.

XM1014 | Incinegator

“Boom. Bam. Bop… Pow.”

Rarity: Classified

Finish Style: Custom Paint Job

Float value range: 0.14 to 0.65

StatTrak™: yes

Price (MW): $12.20

Here is a perfect concept design for a shotgun skin. XM1014 | Incinegator was created by EGO DEATH, and this skin got into the game in The Prisma Collection alongside other community-made finishes in March 2019.

The body of the weapon is decorated with an alligator’s mouth spitting fire. The creator used shades of green, yellow, and brown for the skin’s color palette.

USP-S | Caiman

Rarity: Classified

Finish Style: Custom Paint Job

Float value range: 0.00 to 0.40

StatTrak™: yes

Price (FN): $48.40

USP-S | Caiman is the second item created by OniLolz and Zaphk in our reptiles-themed list of CS:GO skins. It was included in The Huntsman Collection which was released in June 2014. The skin features a red eye of a dark teal caiman on the pistol’s slide, a maroon insert on the magazine, and a solid black silencer.

P250 | See Ya Later

“In a while…”

Rarity: Covert

Finish Style: Custom Paint Job

Float value range: 0.00 to 0.70

StatTrak™: yes

Price (FN): $11.50

P250 | See Ya Later was released as part of The Spectrum 2 Collection in September 2017. The creators of cartoon-styled P250 skin, ClegFX, wewantrox, and dannytrejo4.0, were inspired by Bobby Charles’ song “See Ya Later Alligator”.

The entire pistol is covered with a painted green mechanized crocodile with gray teeth. There are also several yellow mechanical details.

AUG | Chameleon

“Subtlety can make all the difference.”

Rarity: Covert

Finish Style: Custom Paint Job

Float value range: 0.00 to 0.50

StatTrak™: yes

Price (FN): $216

This one will help you slip through unnoticed. AUG | Chameleon , created by OniLolz and Zaphk, first appeared in Counter-Strike: Global Offensive as a part of The Phoenix Collection when Operation Phoenix started in February 2014.

The green chameleon sitting comfortably on a stem and catching an insect with his tongue is depicted on the rifle’s black-painted body. You can also spot a dark green pattern of overlapping arcs in the background.

M4A1-S | Welcome to the Jungle

“Strike without warning.”

Rarity: Covert

Finish Style: Custom Paint Job

Float value range: 0.00 to 1.00

StatTrak™: no

Price (FT): $1,558

M4A1-S | Welcome to the Jungle is a Valve-made skin that was released with The Ancient Collection alongside Operation Broken Fang. The assault rifle is painted in shades of gray, green, and pink. The body of M4A1-S is complemented by a golden pit viper on the background of leaves.

There are also red and pink accents in the form of ancient-styled iconography on the rifle’s magazine, buttstock, and silencer.

AWP | Desert Hydra

“Cutting off one head means two more appear—is that a risk you’re willing to take? – Valeria Jenner, Revolutionary.”

Rarity: Covert

Finish Style: Gunsmith

Float value range: 0.00 to 0.60

StatTrak™: no

Price (FT): $1,935

Created by 2minds studio, AWP | Desert Hydra was released when Operation Riptide started in September 2021. Over the light yellow and peach background with various ornaments, the skin is custom-painted with golden snakes creeping from the buttstock toward the AWP’s barrel.

The eyes and mouths of the snakes are neon cyan. The buttstock, scope, and barrel are covered in white paint and thin brown patterns of lines.

Reptile-Themed CS:GO Gloves Skins

There are two collections of CS:GO skins for fingerless gloves that match the theme of our list: Broken Fang Gloves and Hydra Gloves. Let’s take a closer look at eight reptile-themed gloves you can combine with weapon skins from our list.

Broken Fang Gloves

CS:GO players first got to try on the Broken Fang Gloves when Operation Broken Fang started, and the eponymous skin case was released at the beginning of December 2020.

List of Broken Fang Gloves:

  • Broken Fang Gloves | Yellow-banded .
  • Broken Fang Gloves | Unhinged .
  • Broken Fang Gloves | Jade .
  • Broken Fang Gloves | Needle Point .

These gloves are adorned with metal studs located around the bulky Operation Broken Fang logos. In this collection, you can find black and yellow, black, emerald green, and brown gloves.

Hydra Gloves

Hydra Gloves have been in the game since February 2018. There are black and green, snakeskin and brown, khaki mesh and leather, and brass and gray gloves.

List of Hydra Gloves skins:

  • Hydra Gloves | Emerald .
  • Hydra Gloves | Rattler .
  • Hydra Gloves | Mangrove .
  • Hydra Gloves | Case Hardened .

These gloves are made of leather and synthetic mesh, and are decorated with brass studs and Operation Hydra logos. You can find all of them in The Clutch Case.

Other gloves

We can’t forget to mention Driver Gloves | King Snake : extraordinary gloves crafted with white leather and snakeskin. They will be a perfect match for AWP | Desert Hydra.

If you are not into white skins, get Driver Gloves | Diamondback . These are made of black and tan leather, and snakeskin inserts.

Hand Wraps | Constrictor are much more casual than other gloves here but still attention-worthy. These fingerless gloves are made of brown fabric and tape with a pattern that resembles the skin of a python.
