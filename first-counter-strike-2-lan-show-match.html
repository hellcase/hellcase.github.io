Highlights From ESL‚Äôs First-Ever Counter-Strike 2 Show Match

The Counter-Strike 2 show match began with players of The Beta Boys and Team 27015 entering the scene.

After the captains of both teams shook their hands, players prepared for the first-ever Counter-Strike 2 LAN show match to start.

The Limited Test of Counter-Strike 2 began a few days ago after Valve announced the update would launch in the summer of 2023. Right now, the only map available to play in Counter-Strike 2 is de_dust2, this is why the CS2 show match before the ESL Pro League Season 17 Grand Final was played on this map.

The Beta Boys started the match on the T side, and Team 27015 was playing on the CT side. The pistol round ended with a quick win for Xizt‚Äôs team.

A few rounds later, TeSeS performed a clutch solely winning the round against his two opponents from Team 27015, shalfey and dycha. Madsen used AK-47 | Legion of Anubis.

In the fifth round of the show match, NertZ got a double kill with his AWP | Dragon Lore and then threw a grenade into the smoke to get a vision on the opponent and secure a triple kill.

In the next round, Krad smoked the Mid Doors and then threw a grenade to peek behind the doors, where he noticed Snappi and took him down immediately.

Krad killed two opponents pushing from A Short with his Galil AR, then hit another with a grenade.

Later into the first half of the historical Counter-Strike 2 show match, JustHarry from The Beta Boys was left alone to clutch the round against shalfey, Snappi, and Xizt. Spoiler alert: he nailed the round using his M4A1-S | Emphorosaur-S.
