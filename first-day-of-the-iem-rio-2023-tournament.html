What is the IEM Rio 2023?

What is this tournament? The Intel Extreme Masters (IEM) tournament series began in 2008. Its 18 season consists of three tournaments: IEM Rio 2023, IEM Dallas 2023, and IEM Cologne 2023.

IEM Rio 2023: Format

Sixteen teams are taking part in the tournament, and they are competing using a fairly simple system. Firstly, two groups were formed in a Double Elimination format, with eight teams each. Then, the three best teams from each group moved on to the Playoff Stage. The tournament prize pool is $250,000, and the winner will receive $100,000 and a ticket to IEM Cologne 2023.
