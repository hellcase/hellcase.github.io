What Was My Spring Loadout About?

But before let‚Äôs have a quick overview of the competition itself. My Spring Loadout was an exciting competition from Hellcase where players could show their drawing skills and win a skin. There were seven winners in total. Contestants compete for four types of skins:

  • AK-47 | Fuel Injector (MW)
  • AWP | Containment Breach (MW)
  • M4A1-S | Hyper Beast (FN)
  • USP-S | Neo Noir (FN)

Four participants of the contest could become the owners of the USP-S | Neo-Noir (FN) skin. The rules of the Hellcase Loadout event are very interesting, especially for those who enjoy drawing. The contestant had to come up with the perfect spring loadout and draw it by hand. Users were welcome to draw a real or fictional item.

Participants of the competition had to publish their work on their Twitter or Facebook page with the hashtag #Hellcaseloadout23. Users were required to retweet the post and tag 3 friends in the comments. The deadline for posting works was March 23.
