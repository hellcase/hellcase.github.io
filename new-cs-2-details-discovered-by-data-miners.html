Custom Graffiti Sprays

So far, the only community-made cosmetic items in CS:GO were weapon finishes and stickers. The latest findings suggest that when Counter-Strike 2 comes out, artists will be able to create custom graffiti sprays and upload them to the Steam Workshop.

Custom sprays used to be and are still popular thing on public servers in Counter-Strike 1.6 and Counter-Strike: Source. Custom sprays are easy to make and upload in these games, and are a great way to add a little fun to your gaming experience.

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, however, does not offer a feature for uploading custom graffiti sprays. The only sprays you can use in-game are Valve-made, and you can receive them as in-game drops and buy them on Steam Market or third-party websites.

Also, unlike CS 1.6 and CS:S sprays, the CS:GO sprays are consumable. You can only use a spray 50 times before you need to get a new one.

Although you will most likely not be able to use custom graffiti sprays in Counter-Strike 2, it is still great that community-made sprays may be presented in the Steam Workshop and eventually added to the game.

Do you have any ideas for a new custom graffiti spray already? Get ready because it seems that Steam Workshop will be flooded with those after Counter-Strike 2 launches in the summer.
