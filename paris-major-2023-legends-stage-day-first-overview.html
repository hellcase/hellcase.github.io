Round 2 Matches

Based on the team’s results, they were divided into the upper and lower part of the bracket. The teams that emerged victorious in the Round 2 High Matches earned the chance to compete for the Champions Status and secure their spot in the BLAST.tv Paris Major 2023: Champions Stage.

Conversely, the teams that found themselves in Round 2 Low Matches will face a battle for survival.

Below, you can see the outcomes of these matches.

Round 2 High Matches

Monte and Team Liquid were the first to reach the upper bracket (2:0 pool). The teams faced strong opponents, and the matches were intense, but they managed to secure the victories. Monte first defeated FURIA (16:7 on Ancient) and then had a hard-fought win over Fnatic (19:17 on Vertigo).

Team Liquid first faced 9INE (16:9 Overpass) and then played against NAVI on Inferno, beating them with a 16:8 score.

The situation was somewhat more accessible for Heroic and Vitality, as they were matched against weaker opponents and quickly secured a victory, advancing to the 2:0 pool.

Heroic, Liquid, Vitality, and Monte are just one win away from securing a spot in the Champions Stage.

Round 2 Low Matches

The winning teams from Round 2 Low Matches advanced to the middle bracket (1:1 pool). In the meantime, the teams that suffered losses entered the lower bracket (0:2 pool), where they will battle for survival.
