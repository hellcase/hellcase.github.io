S1mple’s Outstanding Skills & Knife Frags

A titan of the esports scene, Oleksandr ‘s1mple’ Kostyliev is a professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player from Ukraine. He is widely regarded as one of the best CS:GO players in the world and is known for his exceptional skills.

S1mple has played for several top teams, including Natus Vincere, FlipSid3 Tactics, and Team Liquid. He has won numerous tournaments and broke the record for most MVP awards received. He’s the kind of player who can make your jaw drop with his incredible accuracy and reaction time.

He is also famous for his knife frags. There are a lot of clips with him both sneaking on his enemies and winning face-to-face knife battles, and we picked the best of those to share with you. Here are seven clips with s1mple’s knife frags and skins he used.

Butterfly Knife | Crimson Web

In the IEM Global Challenge 2020 Group Stage, NAVI faced FURIA in a BO3 match that ended in a 2:0 win for s1mple’s team. On Nuke, the Ukrainian pro player performed an amazing double kill with his Butterfly Knife | Crimson Web .

Butterfly Knife | Crimson Web was released in Operation Breakout Weapon Case in July, 2014. It has a solid maroon base, a black spider pattern applied to its blade, and a semi-gloss topcoat. This knife has a Hydrographic finish style.

“Be careful where you walk, you never know where the web is spread”.

Rarity: Covert.

Finish Style: Hydrographic.

Float value range: 0.06 to 0.80.

Starting price: $700.

Battle-Scarred variants of Butterfly Knife | Crimson Web sell for around $700, Minimal Wear ones – for $1,800, and Factory New ones – for $6,000. Get this skin for $240 by opening a Prince Case , or go for the Knives Case that costs $107.

Stiletto Knife | Case Hardened

NAVI played against Team Vitality in the PGL Major Antwerp 2022 Europe RMR A Group Stage. In the first map of the BO3, Inferno, s1mple made a nasty kill on the unsuspecting apEX trying to save his weapon.

Stiletto Knife | Case Hardened is a popular knife skin that was added to the Horizon Case in August, 2016. Its blade has been color case-hardened, which resulted in yellow, blue, and purple stains.

“A little color never hurt anyone”.

Rarity: Covert.

Finish Style: Patina.

Float value range: 0.00 to 1.00.

Stiletto Knife | Crimson Web was released in the Horizon Case in August, 2016. The blade of the knife is covered with a solid dark red paint and a spider web pattern all over it, and its handle is black.

Karambit | Doppler (Ruby)

Did you know that MLG CS:GO NA Major Championship: Columbus was the first CS:GO Major event to feature a $1,000,000 prize pool? At that tournament, s1mple played in an All-Star show match between Team America and Team Europe, and he delivered a ridiculously good highlight by killing armed NiKo and kennyS with his Karambit | Doppler (Ruby) only.

Karambit | Doppler Ruby is one of the most expensive skins in CS:GO, and it was released in Chroma Case in January, 2015. The blade of this knife is coated with metallic paints and a marbleizing medium and decorated with a vivid red pattern resembling a ruby’s texture.

“Getting lost in its color can prove fatal”.

Rarity: Covert.

Finish Style: Anodized Multicolored.

Float value range: 0.00 to 0.08.

StatTrak™ is available: yes.

Starting price: $6,900.

Karambit | Doppler is only available in Factory New and Minimal Wear conditions. Although common patterns of this skin cost around $1,300, the Ruby Phase is much more expensive. You can get a Minimal Wear Ruby variant for $6,900 or go for a Factory New Ruby skin that sells for $9,000 or more.

Save your money and claim this skin from the Superconductor Case , which costs $299 only.

Karambit | Case Hardened

Day 4 of the IEM Cologne 2022 was the day when NAVI won a BO3 against Ninjas in Pyjamas within the Group A Upper Bracket Semi-final. On the second map of the series, s1mple sneaked on REZ and stabbed him from behind with his Blue Gem Karambit | Case Hardened .

Karambit | Case Hardened is one of the oldest skins in the game that was brought alongside the Arms Deal update in August, 2013. Common patterns feature yellow, purple, and blue stains on the knife’s blade. However, the rarest patterns, so-called Blue Gems, are mostly blue. These patterns are the most sought-after and, thus, are the most expensive ones.

“A little color never hurt anyone”.

Rarity: Covert.

Finish Style: Patina.

Float value range: 0.00 to 1.00.

StatTrak™ is available: yes.

Starting price: $800.

Common patterns of Karambit | Case Hardened cost $800 to $1,100. The Blue Gem variants are rare, and their price tags start at $20,000 for a Factory New condition. Get this knife from the $15 Hardened Case or the $152 Shiny Knives Case .

Butterfly Knife | Fade

NAVI fans will remember IEM Katowice 2020 Grand Final as one of the greatest series in the team’s history. The Born to Win dominated the server and defeated G2 with a 3:0 score. Fun fact: NAVI led the first half 14:1 on not one, but two maps: Nuke and Mirage.

On the last map of the Grand Final, Mirage, s1mple did not succeed in knifing an enemy at first, but quickly after killed AMANEK with his Butterfly Knife | Fade to secure the round.

Butterfly Knife | Fade is truly one of the best Butterfly Knives in CS:GO. Valve released it when Operation Breakout started within the eponymous weapon case in July 2014. The knife’s blade is adorned with airbrushing transparent paints that create a beautiful red, purple, and orange gradient pattern.

“This isn’t just a weapon, it’s a conversation piece – Imogen, Arms Dealer In Training”.

Rarity: Covert.

Finish Style: Anodized Airbrushed.

Float value range: 0.00 to 0.08.

StatTrak™ is available: yes.

Starting price: $3,300.

Butterfly Knife | Fade is rather pricey. Minimal Wear variants sell for around $3,300, and Factory New ones cost $3,500 minimum. You can get this skin for $145 only by claiming it from the Samsara Case .

Nomad Knife | Case Hardened was released within the operation-exclusive Shattered Web Case that came out in November, 2019. While the handle of the knife is untouched, the blade is covered in yellow, blue, and purple stains.

“A little color never hurt anyone”.

Rarity: Covert.

Finish Style: Patina.

Float value range: 0.00 to 1.00.

StatTrak™ is available: yes.

Common variants of Battle-Scarred Nomad Knife | Case Hardened sell for $300 and for $600 in Factory New condition. Blue Gems, however, cost a minimum of $3,999. Open the Prestige Case to get this skin for $30 only.
