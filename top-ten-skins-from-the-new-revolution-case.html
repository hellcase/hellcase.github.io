What is the Revolution Case?

Revolution Case was released alongside the Espionage Sticker Capsule and the Denzel Curry ULTIMATE Music Kit within the “Case, Capsule, Kit, Oh My!” update that brought the first cosmetics of 2023. We talked about this update here .

In this new case, you can find tons of great skins to add to your arsenal, seventeen of them to be exact. Although the Revolution Collection is full of vivid-colored cosmetics with futuristic and anime-themed patterns, there are also skins for those players who prefer more classy and darker designs.

Revolution Case features seven Mil-Spec skins, five Restricted items, three Classified finishes, and two Covert ones. As for the Rare Special Items, this case might drop you skin for gloves from the Clutch Case if you are lucky enough.

Let’s take a closer look at the ten best CS:GO skins from the Revolution Case without further ado.

SCAR-20 | Fragments

“Fragment of your imagination.”

Rarity: Mil-Spec.

Finish Style: Gunsmith.

Float value range: 0.00 to 0.78.

Price (FN): $2.20

StatTrak™ is available: yes.

This skin for SCAR-20 is adorned with a geometric fragmental pattern with a volumetric illusion that gives it a 3D effect. The color scheme of SCAR-20 | Fragments is well-composed and consists of metallic silver, dark grey, and bronze accents. The author of this weapon finish mentioned that they were inspired by the Polygonal Parametrics style used in graphic design and architecture.

MP9 | Featherweight

“A good marksman doesn’t blame their tools.”

Rarity: Mil-Spec.

Finish Style: Custom Paint Job.

Float value range: 0.00 to 1.00.

Price (FN): $2.60.

StatTrak™ is available: yes.

There is beauty in simple things, and the minimalistic MP9 | Featherweight proves that. Although the skin’s texture is plain, the author made it look like the black-and-white plastic body of the SMG was made with a mesh by cutting hexagonal holes into it. That kind of mesh is used to lighten the item’s weight.

MAG-7 | Insomnia

“Gulp, gulp, gulp.”

Rarity: Mil-Spec.

Finish Style: Custom Paint Job.

Float value range: 0.00 to 1.00.

Price (FN): $2.80.

StatTrak™ is available: yes.

The red-and-black MAG-7 | Insomnia is a custom-painted skin for a popular shotgun, and it was initially created for the Dreams & Nightmares contest. On the gun’s body, the authors depicted a human with red eyes, long black hair, fangs drowning in the black water, and several fishes swimming into the person’s mouth – quite frightening but eye-catching art.

MP5-SD | Liquidation

“Quiet reflection.”

Rarity: Mil-Spec.

Finish Style: Custom Paint Job.

Float value range: 0.00 to 1.00.

Price (FN): $2.75.

StatTrak™ is available: yes.

MP5-SD | Liquidation stands out from all the Mil-Spec skins in the Revolution collection because of the glowing ambient outlines that emphasize the neat form of the gun. The dark blue-and-purple base matches perfectly with red and blue line accents.

P90 | Neoqueen

“Custom crafted for all your rushing needs.”

Rarity: Restricted.

Finish Style: Gunsmith.

Float value range: 0.00 to 0.60.

Price (FN): $11.40.

StatTrak™ is available: yes.

Here comes the color! The first Restricted weapon finishes in our list of the best Revolution Case skins is P90 | Neoqueen. We couldn’t walk past it due to its bold and vibrant design that successfully combines sci-fi and military styles. The P90’s body depicts a woman’s face and a grey snake, complemented with many inscriptions and graffiti. Also, there are animated screens on the gun.

Glock-18 | Umbral Rabbit

“Take a leap of faith.”

Rarity: Restricted.

Finish Style: Custom Paint Job.

Float value range: 0.00 to 0.75.

Price (FN): $13.40.

StatTrak™ is available: yes.

The Asian-styled Glock-18 | Umbral Rabbit has a pink red-eyed rabbit taking a leap of faith in front of the full moon. According to the skin’s creator, the bunny’s name is Peach! Two temples, flying mountains, and delicate peonies complete the picture.

UMP-45 | Wild Child

“The concept is simple, it’s the execution that’s difficult.”

Rarity: Classified.

Finish Style: Custom Paint Job.

Float value range: 0.00 to 1.00.

Price (FN): $60.50.

StatTrak™ is available: yes.

“Not all items need to be new and bright.” – the creator of the UMP-45 | Wild Child . Their skin has a darkened neon-colored base, and there are a lot of sketches and inscriptions on the SMG. The more you look at it, the more details you spot: an arrow and a rocket instead of bullets depicted on a magazine, or “PIU PIU”, “UMP – SKIN FOR CS”, “CLUTCH OR KICK” writings, or an infinity of awesome icons.

AWP | Duality

“There’s two sides to every story.”

Rarity: Classified

Finish Style: Gunsmith.

Float value range: 0.00 to 0.70.

Price (FN): $145.

StatTrak™ is available: yes.

What’s that and where is AWP | Doodle Lore, you might ask. Well, this skin was replaced with AWP | Duality due to copyright infringement. Instead, we got a masterpiece inspired by traditional Japanese style. The author depicted two giant snakes on different sides of the weapon’s body and complimented the skin with elegant golden ornaments and red flowers in the background.

AK-47 | Head Shot

“Everyone has goals, but are you willing to put in the work to achieve them? – Booth, Arms Dealer.”

Rarity: Covert

Finish Style: Custom Paint Job.

Float value range: 0.00 to 1.00.

Price (FN): $385

StatTrak™ is available: yes.

If you are a big fan of Holo stickers, you can now place them on your AK-47 | Head Shot to create matching and great-looking crafts. Underneath the pearlescent coating that gave this skin a holographic effect, a graffiti-styled “HEAD SHOTGUN” inscription in metallic grey color was applied.

M4A4 | Temukau

“Stand your ground!”

Rarity: Covert

Finish Style: Custom Paint Job.

Float value range: 0.00 to 0.80.

Price (FN): $625.

StatTrak™ is available: yes.

M4A4 | Temukau depicts a female CT agent drawn in an anime style which counters a T rush. She stands her ground! The Japanese inscription “temukau” on the gun’s body means “confront”, and the phrase under it we can translate as “they are coming” or “they are here”. There is a chicken icon on the M4A4’s barrel, and the writing next to it says “cute chicken”.
