CS:GO Skins To Match Valentine’s Day Spirit With

CS:GO skins are one-of-a-kind gifts for Valentine’s Day. They are unique, valuable, and make your loved one feel special while they play the game they love with the person they love.

Skins allow you to show your loved one that you admire their passion for the game and have put thought into finding something that they will appreciate. Some skins can be worth thousands of dollars, making them a rare and special gift that your loved one will treasure. This makes it a great gift for skin collectors who value rarity.

Also, using skins can make playing the game more enjoyable. When you give someone a skin, they can feel proud of and show off their unique skin on the server.

When you are choosing a skin to gift, make sure it’s the right weapon for your significant other’s playstyle . We assembled several options of CS:GO skin combos and stickers for couples, so let’s start without further ado.

AUG | Anodized Navy & AUG | Hot Rod

These are true gems for those who admire accuracy and simple designs. AUG | Hot Rod was one of the first skins in the game, added in August, 2013. A month later, Valve released AUG | Anodized Navy alongside Operation Bravo in September, 2013.

Described as “mementos from Ali”, these Mil-Spec skins offer a minimalistic but eye-pleasing design. Bodies of both assault rifles are covered in anodized effect chrome paints: dark blue and maroon. The magazines are solid gray, and the scopes and barrels are untouched.

These skins for AUG are only available in Factory New and Minimal Wear conditions as their float value range are 0.00 to 0.08. In Factory New condition, you can get AUG | Anodized Navy for $18 and AUG | Hot Rod for $278.

AUG | Amber Fade & MAC-10 | Amber Fade

MAC-10 | Amber Fade is a skin from The Mirage Collection. Players first got their hands on it in November, 2013. AUG | Amber Fade is much “younger”, as it was released when Operation Riptide case started in September, 2021.

These Anodized Airbrushed skins are Mil-Spec grade, and both feature smooth gradient transitions with bronze, golden, and patina-styled copper colors. This effect was created by applying translucent paints on the chromed bodies of the guns.

Their pattern index affects the positioning of the gradient pattern on the weapon. Their float value range varies from 0.00 to 0.40, meaning you can acquire them in any condition but Battle-Scarred. A Factory New variant of MAC-10 | Amber Fade will cost you $12, while a Field-Tested one – is $6.60. AUG | Amber Fade sells for $0.80 in Factory New condition and for $0.25 in Field-Tested condition.

AUG | Flame Jörmungandr & Desert Eagle | Emerald Jörmungandr

Made by Valve, AUG | Flame Jörmungandr and Desert Eagle | Emerald Jörmungandr are Restricted-graded skins that were released as a part of The Norse Collection in November, 2019.

Both skins have an Anodized Multicolored finish style. They are decorated with wavy lines that resemble the World Serpent Jörmungandr from Scandinavian mythology and with several ornaments inspired by Norse culture. The color scheme of the AUG skin is yellow, orange, and brown, while the Desert Eagle skin features green, black, and blue.

AUG | Flame Jörmungandr will cost you $210 in Factory New condition and $156 in Well-Worn condition. For a Factory New Desert Eagle | Emerald Jörmungandr, you will have to pay $210, and $170 for a Well-Worn variant.

AUG | Arctic Wolf & UMP-45 | Arctic Wolf

Both AUG | Arctic Wolf and UMP-45 | Arctic Wolf are community-made skins with a Restricted grade. Created by blossomalex, the UMP-45 skin got into the game as a part of The Clutch Collection in February, 2018, and the AUG skin – when Operation Shattered Web started in November, 2019.

These skins depict the cartoon-styled fur of the white-and-gray arctic wolf on a dark blue background. There are accents of red, in addition to white paw prints. On the buttstocks, handles, and magazines, you can spot scratch marks. The finish style of these skins is Custom Paint Job.

The Arctic Wolf skins look best in Factory New condition, but Well-Worn skins are still tolerable. For a Factory New AUG | Arctic Wolf, you will need to spend around $7, and $2.15 for a Well-Worn one. UMP-45 | Arctic Wolf will cost you even less: $1.20 for a Factory New skin and $0.40 for a Well-Worn skin.

AK-47 | Panthera onca & AK-47 | Jaguar

This combo will be perfect for the wild ones who love playing aggressively. Covert AK-47 | Jaguar was released in July, 2014, and Classified AK-47 | Panthera onca – when Operation Broken Fang took place in December, 2022.

Both skins for AK-47 are in Custom Paint Job finish style, they are decorated with big wild cats on camouflage-styled background: olive and beige on AK-47 | Panthera onca, and dark-green and khaki on AK-47 | Jaguar. These skins are available in all conditions, and both Well-Worn and Battle-Scared variants look like decent options to go for if your budget is tight

A Factory New AK-47 | Panthera onca sells for $296, while a Well-Worn one – is for $159. AK-47 | Jaguar is much cheaper, as its price varies from $119 for a Factory New skin to $24 for a Battle-Scarred skin. The only thing left to decide is who will get the black jaguar and who will get the panthera.

AK-47 | The Empress & M4A4 | The Emperor

AK-47 | The Empress and M4A4 | The Emperor are Covert skins made by OniLolz, Zaphk, and 2Mind for assault rifles. First, the AK-47 skin was released in September, 2017. Two years later, the skin for M4A4 got into The Prisma Collection.

The creators of these skins took inspiration from “The Empress” and “The Emperor” Tarot cards. The AK-47 skin depicts a crowned woman in yellow sun rays. The color scheme of this masterpiece includes red, shades of yellow, dark blue, and white.

The design of the M4A4 skin is similar, but it is adorned with an old crowned man, also surrounded by sun rays, and the colors used to paint the weapon are yellow, bright blue, and white. Both skins are highly detailed with several geometric patterns, flags, etc.

A Factory New AK-47 | The Empress will cost you $105, while a still great-looking Field-Tested one – around $27. M4A4 | The Emperor in Factory New condition sells for $190, and in Field-Tested condition for $17.

AUG | Momentum & UMP-45 | Momentum

AUG | Momentum and UMP-45 | Momentum are Classified as community-made skins created by MultiH and dC^. The SMG skin was released as a part of The Danger Zone Collection in December, 2018, and the AUG skin can be found in The Prisma Collection, added in March, 2019.

These bright-colored skins were custom-painted in various shades of cyan, navy, and peach pink. The bodies of the guns are decorated with arrows, and some of their components feature a blue-pink gradient.

A Factory New AUG | Momentum will cost you $25, and a Field-Tested one – $3. UMP-45 | Momentum costs around $8 in a Factory New condition and $4.25 in Field-Tested condition.

Glock-18 | Water Elemental & P2000 | Fire Elemental

Glock-18 | Water Elemental and P2000 | Fire Elemental are community-made skins created by FLaMmenWerFeR, and released separately in 2014.

If you have not played Fireboy and Watergirl together, are you even a couple? The Glock-18 skin is adorned with a water elemental custom painted in shades of blue on a dark red background with glare, and the P2000 skin features a custom-painted fire elemental on a blue background.

As the float value of these skins rises, the pattern gets darker, and only a few scratches appear, so even Field-Tested and Battle-Scarred variants are still a great option. Glock-18 | Water Elemental in Factory New condition costs $12, and $4.30 in Battle-Scarred condition. A Factory New P2000 | Fire Elemental sells for $16, and a Battle-Scarred one – for $7.

M4A1-S | Printstream & Desert Eagle | Printstream

Created by JTPNZ, these skins for M4A1-S and Desert Eagle are a part of the Printstream skin family, which also includes a skin for USP-S. M4A1-S | Printstream and Desert Eagle | Printstream were included in The Broken Fang Collection, released in December, 2022.

These eye-catching Covert skins are custom-painted in black and white. They are adorned with pearlescent highlights and multiple icons, pictograms, lettering, and “XXXY” inscriptions. There are also pearlescent stripes that make great accents.

A Factory New M4A1-S | Printstream will cost you $390, while a Battle-Scarred one around $90. Compared to that, Desert Eagle | Printstream is much cheaper: it costs $128 in Factory New condition and $50 in Battle-Scarred condition. As the float value of these skins rises, a patina layer becomes more distinct, their pattern darkens, and a few scratches appear.

SCAR-20 | Crimson Web & Desert Eagle | Crimson Web

These skins are a part of the Crimson Web skin family, and if you want to go premium, you can also get the matching knives of your choice. SCAR-20 | Crimson Web was added to CS:GO as a part of The Arms Deal 2 Collection in November, 2013, and Desert Eagle | Crimson Web got into The eSports 2014 Summer Collection in July, 2014.

The Crimson Web skin family is as unique as every person is, including you and your significant other. The black thin web pattern is randomly generated for each Crimson Web skin, meaning no weapon or knife skin is the same. The pattern is painted over the crimson-red base.

A Factory New SCAR-20 | Crimson Web will cost you around $10, and only $2 for this skin in Field-Tested condition. To get Desert Eagle | Crimson Web in Factory New condition, you would have to pay $107, and $6 to get a Field-Tested one.

CS:GO Skins With Flowers

MAC-10 | Aloha

“For the noncommittal.”

MAC-10 | Aloha is a community-made skin in an Anodized Multicolored Finish style. It has been airbrushed with a pattern of red hibiscus and leaves, the background is untouched. In Factory New condition, this skin for MAC-10 sells for $3.

MP9 | Rose Iron

“Beware its many thorns.”

If roses are her favorite, that’s the skin to get her. MP9 | Rose Iron depicts a bright-colored rose on a matte black background and branches in shades of pink and yellow. This Restricted SMG skin costs around $7 in Factory New condition.

AUG | Midnight Lily

“While beautiful, lilies are most often associated with death.”

AUG | Midnight Lily is a Restricted assault rifle skin released in 2019. The AUG’s body is gray and decorated with lilies painted with holographic paints that change in color when you inspect the skin at different angles. The Factory New variant of this skin is worth $215.
